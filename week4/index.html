Perú

Lima is the capital of Peru, located on the country's arid Pacific coast. Despite its preserved colonial center, it is a sprawling metropolis and one of the largest cities in South America. The Larco Museum houses a collection of pre-Columbian art, and the Museum of the Nation traces the history of Peru's ancient civilizations. The Plaza de Armas and the 16th-century cathedral are the heart of Lima's old town.
